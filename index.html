Beef Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp. chili powder
  • 1 tsp. cumin
  • ½ tsp. garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp. dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • 3 Tbsp. flour
  • 3 Tbsp. oil
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 4 oz. can of diced green chilies
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • corn or flour tortillas

Cooking Steps

Prepare enchilada sauce

  1. Combine spices and flour in a small container.
  2. Heat oil over medium-high heat. Add spice/flour mix.
  3. Slowly add chicken stock. Cook at a low simmer for 7-9 minutes. Stir frequently.

Prepare beef filling

  1. Brown ground beef, drain.
  2. Add onion. Cook until almost translucent.
  3. Add green chilies. Cook until warm.

Create enchiladas

  1. Line pan with enchilada sauce.
  2. Fill tortilla shell with beef filling, enchilada sauce, and shredded cheese.
  3. Roll tortilla tightly and fill pan.
  4. Cover with remaining enchilada sauce and shredded cheese.
  5. Cook at 350° F for 20 minutes.

Final steps

  1. Let cool.
  2. Enjoy!

Deviled Eggs

Ingredients

  • Dozen eggs
  • ¼ cup Miracle Whip (or Mayo & ½ tsp. vinegar)
  • 1 tsp. mustard
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • Paprika

Cooking Steps

  1. Hard boil eggs.
  2. Peel eggs. Cut eggs in half length-wise.
  3. Separate yolks into a bowl. Set aside the egg whites.
  4. Mash yolks with a fork.
  5. Add all other ingredients except for paprika.
  6. Add yolk mixture back into whites with a spoon.
  7. Sprinkle eggs with paprika.
  8. Enjoy!

Chili

My chili recipe changes a little bit every time. Below are some tips that can be used with any chili recipe.

  • Most Important! Add broth or beer. You need something to cut into all the heavy tomato flavors.
  • Prepare with than one kind of meat. I typically use a pork/beef mix or pork/turkey mix.
  • Remember that beans are optional. Without getting too controversial (some strongly feel that chili with beans is not chili), my personal preference is to add beans. However, I will sometimes leave them out, and the results are delicious.
  • Use both dried and fresh spices. Chop up an onion, dice some garlic. Add a chopped pepper if you'd like.
  • Don't forget toppings. My standard toppings are Fritos and shredded cheese.
  • Enjoy!
